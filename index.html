Clementine Cleaning is a wellness-focused cleaning service currently serving Winnipeg, Steinbach, and Neepawa.

We understand how important a clean and tidy home is for personal well-being and mental health, and we also understand how difficult it can be to maintain a tidy space.

Our goal is to provide support and relief to our clients. We are happy to offer our services to homeowners and businesses. We are grateful that we have the ability to positively benefit the lives of individuals, staff, and families.

— Ava & Jaden, Owners
